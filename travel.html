Travel & Stay

getting to StrathmerePlan your ride to the farmhouse.

Our wedding will be at The Farmhouse at Strathmere, a country estate in North Gower about 30 minutes south of downtown Ottawa. Please give yourself extra time for rural roads, parking, and the walk from your car to the celebration.

Venue Address

Where to Go On Site

Follow the driveway into Strathmere and look for signs for The Farmhouse. Strathmere has multiple event spaces, so please follow the venue signs and any staff directions when you arrive.

Parking

Complimentary on-site parking is available at Strathmere. The property has gravel and grass areas, so choose shoes you can comfortably walk in and use caution after dark.

Driving from Ottawa

Most guests coming from Ottawa, Barrhaven, Nepean, or Manotick should plan on a 20–40 minute drive, depending on where you are staying and weekend traffic.

Rideshare & Taxis

There will not be a shuttle. Uber, Lyft, and taxis can be limited in North Gower late at night, so we recommend arranging your return ride before the wedding day.

Staying Nearby

Ottawa, Barrhaven, Nepean, and Manotick are practical areas to search for hotels or rentals. We will share any room block information here if one becomes available.

Things To Do

for the weekendMake a weekend of it.

If you are visiting from out of town, here are a few easy ways to spend time around Ottawa before or after the wedding.

Downtown Ottawa

Walk by Parliament Hill, the Rideau Canal, Major’s Hill Park, and the ByWard Market for a classic first visit to the city.

Museums

The National Gallery of Canada, Canadian Museum of Nature, and Canadian Museum of History are good options if you have an afternoon free.

Close to the venue

Manotick is a convenient stop for coffee, a meal, or a short walk by the river if you are staying south of the city.

Practical errands

Barrhaven and Nepean have grocery stores, pharmacies, salons, and larger hotel options within a reasonable drive of Strathmere.
