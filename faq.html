Guest Notes

Are children invited?

We love the little people in our lives, but our wedding day will be an adults-only celebration. Thank you for understanding and making arrangements in advance.

May I bring a guest?

Because of venue capacity and planning details, we’re only able to accommodate the guests named on your invitation. Thank you for understanding.

Is the ceremony unplugged?

Yes. We’re asking guests to put phones and cameras away during the ceremony so everyone can be fully present. We’ll happily share professional photos afterward.

What should I wear?

Formal attire. The celebration is at a countryside venue, so please keep grass, gravel, and September evening weather in mind when choosing shoes and layers.

Will dietary restrictions be accommodated?

Yes. When RSVPs open, there will be a place to share dietary restrictions or allergies so we can pass them along to the venue.

Is there an open bar?

Yes, there will be an open bar.

Can I Uber or taxi from the venue?

Strathmere is outside the city, so rideshare and taxi availability may be limited late at night. Please plan your transportation in advance, especially since there will not be a shuttle.

What happens if it rains?

The ceremony is planned outdoors, weather permitting. If the weather does not cooperate, the venue plan will shift accordingly.
