The Venue

Look at the stars...Our cozy farmhouse just beyond the capital

Built in the early 1860s, the Strathemere farmhouse boasts plenty of romantic details. Enter down a sweeping circular staircase for your first look, enjoy a quiet moment with your betrothed in the upstairs alcove, and say your vows under the canopy of majestic 160-year-old walnut trees. As the sun sets, sneak past the veranda into the Heritage Garden, which is filled with fragrant roses and wildflowers.
